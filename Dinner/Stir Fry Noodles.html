Stir Fry Noodles
    4 servings Total time: 15 minutes      
Home                
 

Ingredients

1 tbsp extra virgin olive oil

1 cup julienned onion (100 g)

1 cup julienned red pepper (100 g)

1 cup julienned green pepper (100 g)

1 cup julienned carrot (100 g)

1 tsp ground ginger

4 tbsp soy sauce

1/4 cup water 

300 g Egg noodles

    

 

Step by step instructions

Heat the oil in a pan, add the julienned vegetables, the ground ginger, 2 tablespoons of soy sauce and the water. 

Cook for 5 minutes over high heat or until the vegetables are ready. 

Cook egg noodles according to package directions. 

Add the noodles and 2 tablespoons of soy sauce. Stir and cook for about 2 minutes more.
