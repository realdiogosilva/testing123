Chicken Fajita
    2 servings Total time: 20 minutes        
Home                  
 

Ingredients:

3 boneless skinless chicken breast

1 medium red bell pepper thinly sliced

1 medium yellow bell pepper thinly sliced

1 medium green bell pepper thinly sliced

1 medium onion thinly sliced

2 Tortilla bread

Step by step instructions

If you want to marinate the chicken add oil, lime juice, chili powder, cumin, paprika, garlic and onion powder to ziplock bag and add chicken for 30 minutes.

Spray a cast iron skillet or a regular skillet with cooking spray.

Add the chicken and cook over medium-high heat until cooked, about 3-4 minutes per side.

Remove the chicken and add the peppers, onions and the reserved marinade to the same pan. 

Slice the chicken into wedges and immediately put back in the pan for 5 minutes.
