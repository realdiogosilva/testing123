Grilled Chicken and Vegetables
    4 servings Total time 25 minutes      
Home              
 

Ingredients:

1 cup broccoli 

1 medium carrot

1 medium potato 

1 tbs olive oil

Salt

4 boneless chicken breast

4 tsp chopped fresh thyme leaves

1 tsp freshly grated lemon zest

1 tsp garlic salt

½ tsp pepper

2 tsp vegetable oil

  

Step by step instructions

Wash and cut the vegetables.

Fill half a pan with water and place the vegetables in it. Place a lid on the pan.

In a bowl, mix thyme, lemon, garlic salt and pepper. 

Coat both sides of chicken with oil, then rub with thyme mixture.

Place chicken on grill over 180°C and cook for 12 - 17 minutes turning once or cook for 10 minutes on an electric grill.
