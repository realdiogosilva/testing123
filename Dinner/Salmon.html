Grilled Salmon
    1 serving Total time: 20 minutes        
Home          

 
 

Ingredients

⅛ cup softened butter

1 tbsp chopped basil (or ¼ tsp dried basil)

½ tsp grated lemon peel

113g asparagus spears

½ medium red pepper

1 tsp olive (or vegetable) oil

¼ tsp red and black pepper mix

½ tsp garlic salt

450g salmon (one piece)

 

    

Step by step instructions

Heat closed contact grill for 5 minutes. 

In small bowl, mix butter, basil and lemon peel; set aside.

In large bowl, toss asparagus spears and bell pepper with 1 teaspoon of the oil and 1/4 teaspoon each of the pepper blend, lemon-pepper seasoning and garlic salt.

 Place vegetables crosswise on grill. Grill 4 to 6 minutes or until vegetables are crisp-tender. 

Cut salmon into 4 serving-size pieces. 

Brush salmon with remaining 1 teaspoon oil; sprinkle with remaining 1/4 teaspoon each of the seasonings. 

Place salmon, skin side down, on grill. Grill 4 to 5 minutes or until salmon flakes easily with fork.
