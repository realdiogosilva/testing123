Berry Yogurt Smoothie
    2 Servings Total Time: 5 minutes      
Home                
 

Ingredients

0.75 cup fresh blueberries

0.75 cup fresh blackberries

150g plain Greek yogurt

1 cup vanilla soy milk

1 banana

    

Healthy Berry Yogurt Smoothie. Protein packed to keep you full throughout the day. A perfect on the go breakfast!

Step by step instructions:

Add all of the ingredients to a blender.

Blend for 30 seconds, stir and blend for an additional 30 seconds.
