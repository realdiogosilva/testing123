Blueberry and Banana Pankcake

    8 Pancakes Total time: 10 minutes      
Home                
 

Ingredients:

200 g self raising flour

1 tsp baking powder

310 ml milk

1 egg

Pinch of salt

Butter or oil

    

Step By Step Instructions

In a large bowl place flour, baking powder and salt.

In a different bowl beat the egg and milk together. Pour the mixture into the first bowl and whisk until there are no lumps. 

Place pan (size depending on the pancake size you want) on medium heat (180o) and grease with butter or oil. 

Pour some of the batter into the pan and leave to cook for about 2 -3 minutes or until bubbles appear on the surface. Slide the spatula under the pancake and flip it over to cook the other side.

Once both sides are done remove from the pan. Add fresh blueberries, banana slices and a bit of syrup on top.
