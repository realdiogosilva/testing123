Egg and Avocado Toast

    1 Serving Total time: 10 minutes        
Home                  
 

Ingredients

0.5 avocado

Salt and pepper

2 slice toast

2 eggs

0.5 tablespoon butter

    

 

Step by step instructions

Mash the avocado in a small bowl and season with salt and pepper.

Toast 2 slices of whole grain in a toaster until golden and crispy.

Heat butter in nonstick skillet over medium-high heat until hot. Break egg into a small bowl, then carefully slip the egg onto the skillet. Repeat for second egg.

Reduce heat to low, cook uncovered for 5-7 minutes until whites are completely set.

Place the mashed avocado over the toast, top with the eggs.
