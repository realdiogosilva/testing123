Lazy Cake
    12 servings Total Time: 10 minutes            
Home                  
 

Ingredients:

100g Butter

Sweetened concentrated milk

Plain biscuits

3 tbsp Cocoa

Pistachio (optional)

    

 

Step by step instructions

In a bowl mix the sweetened concentrated milk, butter, cocoa.

Break the biscuits into small pieces and add to the bowl. Add the pistachio (optional)

Line the loaf pan with plastic wrap or wax paper. Place the mix in to the pan and freeze for a couple of hours.
