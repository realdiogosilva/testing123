Chocolate Chip Cookie
    18 Cookies Total Time: 25 minutes

Step by step instructions
Preheat the oven to 350Â°F and spray 2 baking trays with non-stick spray

In a medium bowl, sift the flour, baking soda and salt together.

In another large bowl, using an electric mixer at medium speed, beat together the butter, and sugars until smooth and mixed together well.

Add the egg and vanilla and mix on low speed until mixed in. Gradually add the flour mixture and continue mixing.

Add chocolate chips and stir with a wooden spoon.

Using a small or large ice cream scoop drop the dough onto the baking tray, 

Bake* the cookies, until the bottoms and edges are lightly browned and the tops feel firm when lightly touched,

*Small cookies bake 8-10 minutes

Large cookies bake 10-13 minutes.
 

Ingredients:

1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour see notes below

1 teaspoon baking soda

1/2 teaspoon salt

1/2 cup unsalted butter (room temperature)

1/2 cup firmly packed light brown sugar

6 tablespoons granulated sugar

1 large egg

1 teaspoon vanilla extract

2 1/4 cups semisweet chocolate chip
