Strawberry Banana Smoothie
    3 servings Total Time: 10 minutes        
Home                  
 

Ingredients:

1 cup Milk

1 cup frozen banana

4 cups frozen strawberries

      

Step by step instructions

Add the milk to the blender

Add the strawberries and frozen bananas

Blend until the smoothie is thick and creamy. 

***If you don’t have a high speed blender add a little more milk to help blend.
