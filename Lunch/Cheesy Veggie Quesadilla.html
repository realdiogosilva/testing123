Cheesy Veggie Quesadilla
    1 Serving Total Time: 10 minutes Vegetarian          
Home                    
 

Ingredients (vegetables can be changed to whatever you want)

1 tomato

½ onion

½ bell pepper

1 cup shredded Mexican blend cheese

2 flour tortillas

1tbs room temperature butter

Pico de gaillo

1 tomato

¼ onion

1 tbs freshly squeezed lime juice

Few sprigs of cilantro

Salt and pepper

    

Step by step instructions:
Chop tomato, onion, bell pepper

Heat the grill to medium heat

Assemble the tortilla by covering half of it’s surface with some cheese then a layer of onions, tomatoes and bell peppers. Finish off with another layer of cheese and then fold it in half.

Spread 1/2 tbs of butter on each side of the tortilla and place it in the grill. Let it cook for 2-3 minutes. Take off grill and cut into 4 pieces.

Repeat for the second tortilla

With pico de gaillo (optional) Chop onions, tomato and cilantro

Add lime juice, salt and pepper and mix it all up
