Pizza Toast
    1 servings Total Time: 10 minutes        
Home                  
 

Ingredients

2 slices toast

2 tablespoons of pizza sauce

½ cup cheese

½ package mini pepperonis

      

Step by step instructions
Preheat oven to 200°C.

Spray a baking sheet with non-stick cooking spray and space the toast slices out on it.

Bake the bread slices for 5 minutes until just starting to toast.

Remove toasted bread from oven and spread each slice with 1 tablespoon pizza sauce, sprinkle with ¼ cup shredded mozzarella cheese and garnish with mini pepperonis. 

Return to oven for 8-10 minutes or until cheese is melted.
