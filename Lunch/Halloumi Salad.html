Halloumi Salad
    3 Servings Total Time: 10 minute Vegetarian      
Home                  
 

Ingredients:

Halloumi

Lettuce

Cucumber

1 onion

150g (canned) corn

150g (canned) chickpeas

1 Red pepper 

Olive oil

      

Step by step instructions

Heat the grill and cut the halloumi into thick slices. Place the halloumi pieces onto the grill until they’re slightly brown.

Cut the halloumi  into small square pieces and add into the salad bowl.

Add the sweetcorn and chickpeas (without their juices) into the salad bowl.

Cut the pepper, cucumber, lettuce and onion and add to the salad bowl.

Add 2 tablespoons of olive oil and mix well.
