Chicken Ranch Wrap
    1 Serving Total Time: 10 minutes        
Home                  
 

Ingredients:

1 cup cooked grilled chicken breast

4 tablespoons ranch dressing

0.5 cup mozzarella cheese

0.25 cup cilantro (optional)

2 8” tortilla

      

Step by step instructions

On a tortilla place Â¼ cup chicken, 1/2 tablespoon ranch, 1 tablespoon of cheese (and 1/2 tablespoon of minced cilantro (optional)) on each tortilla. 

Fold tightly to form a wrap/burrito.

Repeat on the other 3 tortillas.

Coat tortillas with a light layer of oil and cook on the grill for 1-2 minutes on each side.
